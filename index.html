Neural Interface Project

Advancing Brain-Computer Interface Innovation

We are dedicated to creating a functional BCI prototype that translates neural signals into binary code. Our goal is to push the boundaries of human-computer interaction through collaboration across neuroscience, engineering, and computing.
By combining expertise in neural signal processing, device engineering, and software development, we maximize innovation across every dimension of BCI research. SynapseX also prioritizes mentorship and learning, inviting students from all backgrounds to contribute to the development of cutting-edge technology.

Our Approach

We believe that interdisciplinary collaboration is the key to unlocking the potential of brain-computer interfaces. From decoding neural signals to scalable software, SynapseX integrates multiple fields to accelerate progress toward real-world applications.
